Why cat is better than dog?

Cats are affectionate pets that can become your loyal companions, providing emotional support and company. Besides,Interacting with cats can help reduce stress and anxiety, contributing to relaxation and peace of mind. Cats are typically quieter than dogs and don't bark loudly, which helps maintain a peaceful living environment. In addition, cats are relatively independent pets and can take care of themselves when you're not at home, requiring less constant supervision. Furthermore, cats can assist in controlling household pests like mice and cockroaches. Therefore, having a cat can bring many joys and benefits to your life.
